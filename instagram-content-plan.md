# Konten Plan Instagram @nakesproid

## 🎯 Tujuan
1. **Brand awareness** — nakes tau NakesPro ada & ngapain
2. **Lead gen** — traffic ke app.nakespro.id → konversi
3. **Trust building** — portofolio, testimoni, edukasi

## 📌 4 Content Pillars

### 1. Edukasi (40%)
Kenapa nakes butuh website, pentingnya digital presence
Format: Carousel, Reels

### 2. Portofolio & Sosial Proof (30%)
Preview website client, testimoni, case study
Format: Carousel, Reels

### 3. Produk & Proses (20%)
Cara order, milih template, fitur website
Format: Carousel

### 4. Personal & Relate (10%)
Salman sebagai perawat + builder, BTS
Format: Story, Reels

## 🎨 Visual Identity
- Sage #7FA88E, Dusty Blue #A4C2D9, Peach #F4C7A3, Cream #FAF7F2
- Font: Poppins Bold / Inter
- Minimalis, banyak white space

## 📈 Growth Strategy
- Hashtag 3 layer (broad → niche), max 15/post
- Engage akun nakes 10-20/hari
- Link bio → app.nakespro.id
- CTA di setiap post
